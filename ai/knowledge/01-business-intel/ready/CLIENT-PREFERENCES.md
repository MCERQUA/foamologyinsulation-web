## Ad Copy Style Preferences

### Do's:
- Use checkmarks (✔) for bullet lists
- Keep copy short and punchy for social ads
- Focus on problems homeowners don't know they have
- Mention specific issues (disconnected bathroom fans, air leaks, ice dams)
- End with clear CTA: "Schedule your inspection today"
- Use real jobsite photos

### Don'ts:
- NO dashes (-) in bullet points
- Don't make copy too long for Facebook
- Avoid generic insulation messaging

### Approved Short Ad Format:
```
[Problem hook - 1-2 sentences about hidden issues]

[What we find - brief list with checkmarks]

[Brand promise - clear findings, honest recommendations, no pressure]

[CTA - Schedule today]
```
