## Service Bundling Opportunities

### Most Common Service Bundles in Market:

1. **Spray Foam + Air Sealing + Vapor Barrier**
   - Offered by: Most spray foam contractors
   - Value: Comprehensive envelope solution

2. **Thermal Inspection + Spray Foam + Air Sealing**
   - Offered by: Alas-Co, limited others
   - Opportunity: Foamology strength

3. **Attic Insulation + Ice Dam Prevention**
   - Offered by: Several competitors
   - Seasonal: High demand in fall

4. **Crawlspace Encapsulation + Moisture Control**
   - Offered by: Polyseal, some others
   - Value: Complete crawlspace solution

5. **Foundation Lifting + Spray Foam**
   - Offered by: Polyseal exclusively
   - Unique: Combined structural + insulation

### Recommended Bundles for Foamology:

**"Total Home Comfort Package"**
- Thermal inspection
- Spray foam insulation (attic + crawlspace)
- Air sealing
- Ice dam prevention assessment
- Energy savings report

**"Crawlspace Wellness Package"**
- Insulation removal
- Moisture inspection
- Vapor barrier installation
- Spray foam insulation
- Encapsulation completion

**"Pre-Winter Prep Package"**
- Thermal inspection
- Ice dam risk assessment
- Attic air sealing
- Problem area spray foam
- Recommendations report
