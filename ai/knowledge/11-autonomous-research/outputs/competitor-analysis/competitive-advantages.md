## Detailed USP Analysis by Competitor

### 1. Polyseal Insulation / MCGA Alaska
**Primary USP:** "The one-stop shop for insulation AND foundation services"

**Unique Capabilities:**
- Foundation lifting services (rare in market)
- Structural foam applications
- Helical piers and foundation piles
- Concrete slab lifting
- Earthquake damage repair
- Merged with MCGA for expanded services

**Competitive Advantage:**
Polyseal is the ONLY competitor offering both spray foam insulation AND foundation repair services. This creates a unique value proposition for customers with multiple needs.

**Proof Points:**
- SprayFoam 2023 Commercial Enclosure SPF Award (Fort Yukon project)
- Concrete Lifting category award (Whittier Dolphin Cell Repair)
- 19 years in business

**Weakness:**
- Wide service range may dilute insulation focus
- Jack of all trades perception possible

---

### 2. Northern Insulation Solutions
**Primary USP:** "Alaska's Largest Locally-Owned Mechanical Insulation Company"

**Unique Capabilities:**
- Mechanical insulation expertise
- Marine insulation services
- Tank insulation systems
- Custom fabrication
- Industrial project capability
- Remote Alaska project experience

**Competitive Advantage:**
Northern Insulation targets the commercial/industrial market that most residential-focused competitors ignore. Their mechanical insulation expertise makes them the go-to for complex industrial projects.

**Proof Points:**
- Multiple manufacturer partnerships (Owens Corning, 3M, Johns Manville)
- "50+ years combined experience" claim
- Serves urban and remote Alaska

**Weakness:**
- "Largest" claim not independently verified
- Founded 2020 contradicts longevity messaging
- Not BBB accredited

---

### 3. Urethane Alaska LLC
**Primary USP:** "48 years of trusted family service"

**Unique Capabilities:**
- Fireproofing services (unique in market)
- PolyShield protective coating
- Class A 2lb closed-cell foam
- Foam injection (under-foundation)

**Competitive Advantage:**
Urethane Alaska's 48-year history makes them the longest-operating spray foam company in the Alaska market. Family-owned messaging resonates with customers valuing trust and stability.

**Proof Points:**
- BBB A+ rating
- Huntsman Building Solutions partnership
- Since 1977 - pre-dates most competitors by decades

**Weakness:**
- Limited marketing/web presence
- No visible content strategy
- May rely too heavily on reputation alone
