## Detailed Content Analysis

### 1. Chinook Foam & Coating (Best Content Strategy)

**Blog Content:**
- Comprehensive educational articles
- Topics include:
  - "Spray Foam vs Fiberglass: Which is Better for Alaska?"
  - "How Spray Foam Prevents Mold in Crawl Spaces"
  - "Cabin Insulation for Off-Grid Alaska Properties"
  - "Scientific Foundation of Spray Foam Insulation"
- Regular posting schedule evident
- SEO-optimized content structure

**Content Themes:**
1. Alaska-specific challenges
2. Comparison content (spray foam vs alternatives)
3. Problem-solution articles (mold, moisture, energy loss)
4. Remote/cabin-focused content
5. Scientific/educational explanations

**Social Media:**
- Facebook: Active presence
- Instagram: Project photos
- YouTube: Some video content

**Strengths:**
- Best educational content in market
- Niche focus (remote Alaska) well served
- FAQ content addresses common objections
- Scientific credibility established

**Weaknesses:**
- Could use more video content
- Case studies not prominently featured
- Customer testimonial content limited

**Content Frequency:** ~2-4 posts per month (estimated)
