## Licensing Analysis

### Alaska Contractor License Requirements

All insulation contractors in Alaska must have:
1. State contractor license (23 Construction or similar)
2. Business license registration
3. General liability insurance
4. Workers' compensation insurance (if employees)
5. Bonding (recommended)

## Certification Gap Analysis

### What Certifications Are Missing in Market:

1. **SPFA Professional Certification Program (PCP)**
   - Only Turnagain confirmed as SPFA member
   - No competitors prominently display SPFA PCP certification
   - **OPPORTUNITY:** First mover advantage for certified installers

2. **BPI Building Analyst Certification**
   - No competitors display BPI certification
   - Energy efficiency focus increasingly important
   - **OPPORTUNITY:** Differentiate with energy expertise

3. **RESNET HERS Rater**
   - No home energy rating certified companies
   - Ties into new construction and energy codes
   - **OPPORTUNITY:** Add value with energy rating services

4. **EPA Lead-Safe Certification**
   - Not prominently displayed by any competitor
   - Required for work on pre-1978 homes
   - **OPPORTUNITY:** Compliance assurance for older homes

5. **Manufacturer-Specific Certifications**
   - Few competitors display manufacturer training certificates
   - Icynene, Demilec, BASF all offer training programs
   - **OPPORTUNITY:** Display manufacturer certifications prominently
