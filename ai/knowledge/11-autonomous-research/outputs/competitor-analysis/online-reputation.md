## Reputation Themes Analysis

### What Customers Value Most (Positive Triggers):

1. **Communication** (mentioned in 80%+ positive reviews)
   - Clear explanations
   - Responsive to calls/emails
   - Professional interactions

2. **Timeliness** (mentioned in 70%+ positive reviews)
   - On-time arrival
   - Project completion as promised
   - Quick scheduling

3. **Quality Workmanship** (mentioned in 90%+ positive reviews)
   - Professional installation
   - Attention to detail
   - Visible quality improvements

4. **Cleanliness** (mentioned in 50%+ positive reviews)
   - Clean job sites
   - Post-work cleanup
   - Respect for property

5. **Value/Pricing** (mentioned in 40%+ positive reviews)
   - Competitive quotes
   - Fair pricing
   - Value for money

### What Damages Reputation (Negative Triggers):

1. **Poor Communication** (most common complaint)
   - Unresponsive to calls
   - Rude interactions
   - Unclear expectations

2. **Quality Issues** (second most common)
   - Incomplete work
   - Poor installation technique
   - Problems after completion

3. **Timeline Failures**
   - Projects taking too long
   - Missed appointments
   - Delays without communication

4. **Professionalism Lapses**
   - Unprofessional behavior
   - Messy work sites
   - Disrespectful interactions

## Recommendations for Foamology

### Priority 1: Build Review Foundation

**Target Platforms (in order):**
1. Google Business Profile (claim/optimize)
2. Yelp Business Page
3. BBB Accreditation (pursue immediately)
4. Angi Pro Profile
5. Facebook Business Page

**Review Generation Strategy:**
- Ask satisfied customers for reviews
- Follow up after project completion
- Provide easy review links
- Time requests appropriately (not too soon, not too late)

### Priority 2: Review Response Protocol

**Positive Reviews:**
- Thank customer by name
- Mention specific project details
- Invite them to share with friends/family
- Keep response brief but warm

**Negative Reviews:**
- Respond within 24-48 hours
- Acknowledge concern professionally
- Offer offline resolution
- Never argue publicly
- Follow up after resolution

### Priority 3: Reputation Monitoring

**Set Up Alerts:**
- Google Alerts for company name
- Yelp business notifications
- BBB complaint alerts
- Social media mentions

**Regular Monitoring:**
- Weekly review of all platforms
- Monthly reputation report
- Quarterly competitor analysis

### Priority 4: Competitive Positioning

**Leverage Competitor Weaknesses:**
- Weatherize Alaska quality issues = opportunity
- Chinook minimal reviews = gap to fill
- Northern Insulation no BBB = trust differentiator
- Many competitors lack Yelp presence = opportunity

**Build Differentiating Reputation:**
- Emphasize communication excellence
- Document quality workmanship
- Showcase thermal imaging results
- Feature before/after success stories
