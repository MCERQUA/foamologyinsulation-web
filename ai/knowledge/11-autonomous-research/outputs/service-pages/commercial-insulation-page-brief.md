### 6. Frequently Asked Questions (300-350 words)

**Q: How much does commercial insulation cost?**
A: Commercial projects typically range from $25,000-150,000 depending on building size and scope. Smaller office spaces may be less. We provide detailed quotes after site assessment.

**Q: How long will commercial insulation take?**
A: Timelines range from 2-3 days for small spaces to 1-2 weeks for large facilities. We work to minimize business disruption.

**Q: Can you work around our business operations?**
A: Yes. We schedule around your needs—nights, weekends, or phased approaches. Many projects complete with minimal operational impact.

**Q: Will spray foam stop condensation in my metal building?**
A: Yes. Closed cell spray foam eliminates condensation by preventing warm interior air from contacting cold metal surfaces. This is one of the most common reasons for commercial spray foam.

**Q: Is spray foam fire-rated?**
A: Our spray foam is Class 1 fire-rated. For occupied commercial spaces, it's typically covered with thermal barrier (drywall) per building code. We advise on code requirements for your specific application.

**Q: Do you work on multi-story buildings?**
A: Yes. We have equipment and experience for multi-story commercial projects.

**Q: What about warranty?**
A: We provide workmanship warranty on all installations. Manufacturer product warranties also apply (typically 15-25 years).

**Q: Can spray foam be applied in cold weather?**
A: Yes. Our equipment allows application in Alaska conditions. Extremely cold weather may require temporary heating of work area.
