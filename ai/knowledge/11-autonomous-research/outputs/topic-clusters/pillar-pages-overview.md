- **SEO Elements:**
  - Keyword-optimized title (60 chars)
  - Meta description (155 chars)
  - H1 with primary keyword
  - H2s with secondary keywords
  - Image alt text
  - Internal links to cluster pages

  - Table of contents
  - Jump links for sections
  - Visual elements (images, infographics)
  - Callout boxes for key info

  - Contact form embed
  - Phone number prominently displayed
  - Free estimate CTA
  - Trust signals (reviews, certifications)
  - Before/after photos where applicable
