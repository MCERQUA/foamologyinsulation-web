## Opportunity Scoring Methodology

| Factor | Weight | Description |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Traffic Potential | 25% | Monthly search volume that could be captured |
| Conversion Intent | 30% | How likely traffic is to convert |
| Competition Gap | 20% | How weak competitor coverage is |
| Effort Level | 15% | Resources required to create |

**Overall Score = (Traffic x 0.25) + (Conversion x 0.30) + (Competition Gap x 0.20) + (Ease x 0.15) + (Strategic x 0.10)**
