## Executive Summary

Analysis of 10 major Alaska insulation competitors reveals that Foamology is missing significant content that competitors use effectively. However, there are also major opportunities where **NO competitor** has strong content, presenting first-mover advantages.

### Key Findings:
- **Chinook Foam & Coating** leads in educational blog content
- **Turnagain Spray Foam** leads in social proof and testimonials
- **NO competitor** has strong video content (MASSIVE opportunity)
- **NO competitor** has interactive tools/calculators
- **NO competitor** effectively covers AHFC rebate information
