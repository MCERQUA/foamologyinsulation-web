## Alaska Climate Context

Alaska experiences some of the most extreme temperature variations in the United States:
- **Winter temperatures:** -40°F to 20°F (Interior can reach -60°F)
- **Heating season:** September through May (8-9 months)
- **Average annual heating degree days:** 10,000+ (vs. 4,000-6,000 for lower 48)
- **Fuel costs:** 60% higher than US average; rural areas 2-3x higher

## Notes

- Winter keyword volume is 3-4x higher than summer in Alaska
- Ice dam keywords spike dramatically December-February
- Emergency keywords have highest conversion rates
- Off-season (summer) offers opportunity for lower competition
- Weatherization program keywords peak in fall
- Heating cost keywords rise with fuel prices
- Content should be published 4-6 weeks before seasonal peak
