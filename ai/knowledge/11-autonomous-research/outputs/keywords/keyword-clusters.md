## Recommendations

### Priority Content Creation
1. **Ice Dam Prevention Pillar** - Unique Alaska focus, low competition
2. **Cost/Pricing Pillar** - Highest search intent
3. **Comparison Articles** - Capture decision-stage buyers
4. **Location Pages** - Local SEO foundation
5. **Thermal Inspection Content** - Differentiator

### Internal Linking Rules
1. Every blog post links to relevant service page
2. Service pages link to related services
3. All content links to contact/quote page
4. Location pages link to service pages
5. Comparison pages link to both compared services

### Content Refresh Schedule
- Pillar pages: Quarterly review
- Blog posts: Annual update
- Pricing content: Monthly verification
- Location pages: Bi-annual review
- Seasonal content: Pre-season refresh

---

## Notes

- Each cluster should have minimum 5-10 supporting content pieces
- Pillar pages should be 2,000+ words
- Supporting content 800-1,500 words
- Every page should target 1 primary keyword + 2-3 secondary
- Internal links should use keyword-rich anchor text
- Update older content to link to new content
