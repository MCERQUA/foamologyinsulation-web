## Content Quality Standards

### Word Count Minimums
- Location pages: 1,500+ words
- Blog articles: 1,200+ words
- Comprehensive guides: 2,500+ words
- FAQ pages: 2,000+ words

### Required Elements
- Target keyword in title, H1, first paragraph
- Secondary keywords throughout
- Local references (city names, landmarks, climate)
- Internal links to related pages
- External links to authoritative sources
- Images with alt text
- Clear CTAs
- Schema markup

### Local SEO Elements
- City/region in title tag
- Geographic references in content
- Local landmarks or references
- Service area mentions
- Local customer testimonials when available
