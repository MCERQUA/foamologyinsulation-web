## Link Quality Guidelines

### Good Links (Pursue)
- Relevant industry websites
- Local business directories
- Government and educational sites
- High-authority news sites
- Chamber and association member pages
- Manufacturer dealer networks

### Avoid (Low Quality)
- Link farms and directories
- Irrelevant website categories
- Paid link schemes
- Private blog networks (PBNs)
- Low-quality guest post farms
- Spammy directories

### Link Attributes
- Prefer dofollow when possible
- Accept nofollow from high-authority sites
- Ensure NAP consistency in all links
- Include keyword-rich anchor text naturally
