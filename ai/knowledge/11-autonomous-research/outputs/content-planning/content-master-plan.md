## Content Quality Standards

### All Content Must Include:

**SEO Elements**
- Primary keyword in title, H1, meta description
- Secondary keywords in H2s and body
- Internal links to related content (min 3)
- External links to authoritative sources (min 2)
- Image alt text with keywords
- Schema markup where applicable

**User Experience**
- Table of contents for 1,500+ word content
- Bulleted lists and tables for scannability
- Visual elements (images, diagrams, infographics)
- Mobile-responsive formatting
- Fast page load speed

**Conversion Elements**
- Clear CTA above the fold
- Secondary CTAs every 500-800 words
- Phone number visible on every page
- Contact form or quote button
- Trust signals (reviews, certifications)
