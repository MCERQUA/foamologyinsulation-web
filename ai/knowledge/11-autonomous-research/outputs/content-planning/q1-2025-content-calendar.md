## Content Dependencies & Internal Linking Map

### Primary Link Flows

```
Homepage
    |
    +---> Service Areas Hub
    |         |---> All Location Pages
    |         |---> Link to Relevant Service Pages
    |
    +---> Service Pages
    |         |---> Related Blog Posts
    |         |---> Pillar Pages
    |
    +---> Pillar Pages
    |         |---> Cluster Blog Posts
    |         |---> Service Pages
    |         |---> Resource Pages
    |
    +---> Blog Posts
              |---> Related Services
              |---> Parent Pillar
              |---> Other Related Blogs
```

### Mandatory Internal Links

Each content piece must link to:
- **Pillar Pages**: Link to 1-2 related pillars
- **Service Pages**: Link to 1-2 relevant services
- **Location Pages**: Link from service pages, pillar pages
- **Blog Posts**: Link to parent pillar + 2-3 related posts
- **All Pages**: Link to contact/quote page

## Quality Checkpoints

### Pre-Publish Checklist

For each content piece before publishing:

- [ ] Title includes primary keyword (60 characters max)
- [ ] Meta description written (155 characters max)
- [ ] H1 tag with primary keyword
- [ ] H2/H3 structure with secondary keywords
- [ ] Internal links added (minimum 3)
- [ ] External authority links (minimum 1)
- [ ] Images with alt text
- [ ] CTA buttons placed appropriately
- [ ] Mobile preview checked
- [ ] Schema markup added
- [ ] URL structure verified
- [ ] Word count meets minimum

### Weekly Quality Review

Every Friday:
- Review all content published that week
- Check Google Search Console for indexing
- Monitor page load speeds
- Review engagement metrics
- Update internal links as needed
