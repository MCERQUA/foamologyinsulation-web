# Blog Promotion Strategy - Foamology Insulation

**Created:** December 19, 2025
**Purpose:** Define promotion tactics for each content type to maximize reach and engagement

---

## Promotion Strategy Overview

Effective blog content requires strategic promotion to reach target audiences. This document outlines specific promotion tactics for each content type, channel strategies, and measurement approaches for Foamology Insulation's blog content.

### Promotion Goals
1. **Drive Traffic**: Increase organic and referral traffic to blog content
2. **Generate Leads**: Convert readers into quote requests and consultations
3. **Build Authority**: Establish Foamology as Alaska's insulation expert
4. **Support SEO**: Build backlinks and social signals for search rankings
5. **Engage Community**: Connect with local Alaska homeowners and businesses

---

## Channel-Specific Strategies

### Facebook Strategy

**Posting Frequency:** 3-4x per week
**Best Times:** Tuesday-Thursday, 9 AM and 7 PM Alaska Time
**Content Mix:**
- 40% Educational/Tips
- 25% Case Studies/Results
- 20% Seasonal/Timely
- 15% Promotional/CTA

**Tactics:**
- Use native video over YouTube links
- Ask questions in captions for engagement
- Use carousel posts for comparisons
- Boost top-performing organic posts
- Respond to all comments within 24 hours

### Instagram Strategy

**Posting Frequency:** 4-5x per week (feed + stories)
**Best Times:** Monday-Friday, 6-8 PM Alaska Time
**Content Mix:**
- Feed: Before/after, tips carousels, team photos
- Stories: Behind-the-scenes, polls, Q&A
- Reels: Quick tips, project reveals, process videos

**Tactics:**
- Use location tags for Alaska communities
- Create Highlight categories for content types
- Leverage carousel posts for educational content
- Use branded hashtags (#FoamologyInsulation)
- Partner with local Alaska influencers

### LinkedIn Strategy

**Posting Frequency:** 2-3x per week
**Best Times:** Tuesday-Thursday, 7-8 AM and 12 PM
**Content Focus:**
- Industry insights and trends
- Commercial project case studies
- Energy efficiency business case
- Contractor/builder resources

**Tactics:**
- Publish long-form articles for key posts
- Engage with construction/building groups
- Connect with commercial property managers
- Share employee expertise and certifications

### Pinterest Strategy

**Posting Frequency:** 5-10 pins per week
**Board Categories:**
- Home Insulation Tips
- Alaska Home Solutions
- Energy Savings Ideas
- Before & After Transformations
- Seasonal Home Prep

**Tactics:**
- Create tall, text-overlay infographics
- Link all pins directly to blog posts
- Use keywords in pin descriptions
- Join group boards for home improvement
- Repurpose blog graphics as pins

### YouTube Strategy

**Posting Frequency:** 2-4 videos per month
**Content Types:**
- Educational explainers (5-10 min)
- Project walkthrough (3-5 min)
- Quick tips/Shorts (60 sec)
- Customer testimonials (2-3 min)

**Tactics:**
- Optimize titles and descriptions for search
- Create playlists by topic
- Include CTAs in videos and descriptions
- Embed videos in corresponding blog posts
- Cross-promote on other social channels

### Email Marketing Strategy

**Newsletter Frequency:** Bi-weekly
**Segments:**
- Cold subscribers (educational focus)
- Warm leads (comparison/buying focus)
- Past customers (referral/seasonal focus)
- Commercial contacts (business case focus)

**Content Approach:**
- Lead with value, not sales
- Feature one main blog post per newsletter
- Include seasonal reminders
- Personal stories from Magnus/team
- Clear single CTA per email

## Paid Promotion Strategy

### Google Ads

**Campaign Types:**
1. **Search Campaigns** - Target buying-intent keywords
2. **Display Retargeting** - Blog visitors to quote page
3. **YouTube Ads** - Pre-roll on home improvement videos

**Budget Allocation:**
- 60% Search (high intent)
- 30% Retargeting (warm audience)
- 10% YouTube (awareness)

**Blog Post Promotion:**
- Promote buyer guides and comparison content
- Target seasonal content during peak seasons
- Use blog posts as landing pages for educational keywords

### Facebook/Instagram Ads

**Campaign Types:**
1. **Engagement Campaigns** - Boost top organic posts
2. **Traffic Campaigns** - Drive to key blog content
3. **Lead Gen Campaigns** - Gated content/guides
4. **Retargeting** - Website visitors to quote

**Audience Targeting:**
- Alaska homeowners 35-65
- Interest in home improvement, energy efficiency
- Lookalike audiences from customers
- Retargeting from specific blog posts

**Budget Allocation:**
- 40% Retargeting
- 30% Lookalike audiences
- 20% Interest targeting
- 10% Engagement boosting

## Performance Measurement

### Content Performance KPIs

| Metric | Target | Measurement Tool |
|--------|--------|------------------|
| Organic Traffic | +20% monthly | Google Analytics |
| Social Engagement | 5% rate | Platform analytics |
| Email Open Rate | 25%+ | Email platform |
| Quote Requests | +15% monthly | CRM |
| Backlinks Earned | 5+ per month | Ahrefs/SEMrush |
| Social Shares | 50+ per post | Platform analytics |
| Time on Page | 3+ minutes | Google Analytics |
| Featured Snippets | 10+ | Search Console |

### Monthly Reporting Template

1. **Traffic Summary**
   - Total sessions by source
   - Top 10 blog posts by traffic
   - New vs returning visitors

2. **Engagement Metrics**
   - Average time on page
   - Bounce rate by content type
   - Social shares and comments

3. **Conversion Metrics**
   - Quote requests from blog
   - Email signups from content
   - Phone calls attributed to blog

4. **SEO Performance**
   - Keyword ranking changes
   - Featured snippets earned
   - Backlinks acquired

5. **Content Production**
   - Posts published
   - Content repurposed
   - Promotion activities completed
