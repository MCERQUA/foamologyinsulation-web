## Content Types & Formats

### Long-Form Educational Guides (40%)
**Word Count:** 2,000-3,500 words
**Purpose:** Comprehensive topic coverage, pillar content support
**Examples:**
- "Complete Guide to Spray Foam Insulation for Alaska Homes"
- "Understanding R-Values: Everything Alaska Homeowners Need to Know"
- "The Definitive Guide to Crawl Space Insulation in Cold Climates"

**SEO Value:** High - targets multiple keywords, earns backlinks
**Conversion Value:** Medium - builds trust, moves readers to service pages

### Comparison Articles (20%)
**Word Count:** 1,500-2,500 words
**Purpose:** Capture comparison search queries, demonstrate expertise
**Examples:**
- "Closed Cell vs Open Cell Spray Foam: Which is Right for Alaska?"
- "Spray Foam vs Fiberglass: Complete Cost and Performance Comparison"
- "DIY vs Professional Insulation: True Cost Analysis"

**SEO Value:** High - featured snippet opportunities
**Conversion Value:** High - readers are in decision-making phase

### How-To Guides (15%)
**Word Count:** 1,200-2,000 words
**Purpose:** Practical value, builds authority, captures long-tail searches
**Examples:**
- "How to Know When Your Home Needs Better Insulation"
- "How to Prepare Your Home for an Insulation Upgrade"
- "How to Read Your Energy Bills to Find Insulation Problems"

**SEO Value:** Medium-High - good long-tail capture
**Conversion Value:** High - positions us as helpful experts

### Seasonal/Timely Content (10%)
**Word Count:** 1,000-1,800 words
**Purpose:** Capture seasonal search spikes, stay relevant
**Examples:**
- "Preparing Your Alaska Home for Winter: Insulation Checklist"
- "Ice Dam Prevention Guide for Alaska Homeowners"
- "Summer is the Best Time for Insulation Projects (Here's Why)"

**SEO Value:** Medium - seasonal traffic spikes
**Conversion Value:** High - time-sensitive urgency

### Local/Location Content (10%)
**Word Count:** 1,200-2,000 words
**Purpose:** Local SEO, community connection, personalization
**Examples:**
- "Spray Foam Insulation in Anchorage: Climate Considerations"
- "Mat-Su Valley Homes: Insulation Challenges and Solutions"
- "Eagle River Home Insulation Guide"

**SEO Value:** High for local - low competition
**Conversion Value:** Very High - high-intent local searches

### Case Studies & Success Stories (5%)
**Word Count:** 800-1,500 words
**Purpose:** Social proof, real-world results, trust building
**Examples:**
- "Case Study: 40% Heating Bill Reduction in Wasilla Home"
- "Commercial Project: Warehouse Insulation in Anchorage"
- "Historic Home Retrofit: Preserving Comfort in Palmer"

**SEO Value:** Low-Medium - not heavily searched
**Conversion Value:** Very High - demonstrates results

| "ice dam prevention" | 590 | Low | Seasonal content |
| Question keywords | 10,000+ | Low | FAQ and how-to content |

### Featured Snippet Opportunities

Target "Position 0" for these query types:
- **Definition queries:** "What is closed cell spray foam?"
- **Comparison queries:** "Closed cell vs open cell"
- **Process queries:** "How does spray foam insulation work?"
- **List queries:** "Signs you need better insulation"
- **Table queries:** "Spray foam R-value per inch"

**Formatting Requirements:**
- Clear H2/H3 structure
- Bullet points and numbered lists
- Comparison tables
- Definition paragraphs (40-60 words)
- FAQ schema markup

### Internal Linking Strategy

**Every blog post must include:**
1. Link to relevant pillar page (primary)
2. Link to related service page (conversion)
3. Links to 2-3 related blog posts (engagement)
4. Link to contact/quote page (conversion)
5. Link to location page if locally relevant

**Link Anchors:**
- Use keyword-rich anchor text
- Vary anchor text to avoid over-optimization
- Prioritize contextual links within content
- Use "Learn more" or CTA buttons sparingly
