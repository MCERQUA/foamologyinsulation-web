## Case Study Strategy

Case studies are the most powerful conversion content because they demonstrate real results with specific data. They answer the question: "What will this actually do for me?"

### Case Study Elements That Convert

1. **Specific location** (builds trust, local relevance)
2. **Defined problem** (reader identification)
3. **Measurable results** (energy savings %, cost reduction)
4. **Before/after visuals** (thermal images, photos)
5. **Customer voice** (quotes and testimonials)
6. **ROI/payback data** (financial justification)
