## Key Data Points

### Costs (2025)
- Open-cell: $1.00-$3.00/sq ft
- Closed-cell: $1.50-$4.50/sq ft
- Average project: $1,453-$4,335
- Attic: $3,500-$7,000
- 2,000 sq ft house: $4,000-$8,000+

### ROI
- Payback period: 3-7 years
- Energy savings: 30-50%
- Annual savings: $300-$1,000
- Lifespan: 80-100 years

### Performance
- Closed-cell R-value: R-6 to R-7/inch
- Open-cell R-value: R-3.5-3.7/inch
- 20-40% better than fiberglass at equal R-value
- 300% greater racking strength (closed-cell)
