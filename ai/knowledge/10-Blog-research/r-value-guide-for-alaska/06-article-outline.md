## H2: Understanding R-Value: What Alaska Homeowners Need to Know

### H3: What Is R-Value?
- Definition: resistance to heat flow
- Higher = better insulation
- Measurement standard (DOE reference)

### H3: Factors That Affect R-Value Performance
- Insulation type and thickness
- Compression (reduces effectiveness)
- Moisture accumulation
- Thermal bridging through studs/joists
- Age and settling
