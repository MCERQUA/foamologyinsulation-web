## Key Research Findings

### Topic Research Highlights
- Ice dams form when attic temps exceed 30°F and outdoor temps fall below 22°F
- Air sealing is THE most important prevention step (more than insulation alone)
- Spray foam provides dual air sealing + insulation in one application
- Closed-cell spray foam: R-7 per inch, 80-100 year lifespan
- Average ice dam insurance claim: $20,000+
- Alaska homeowners face extreme conditions making prevention critical
