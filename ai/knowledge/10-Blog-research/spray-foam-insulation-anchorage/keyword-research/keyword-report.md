## Market Intelligence Summary

### Alaska-Specific Insights

1. **Climate Drivers**: Alaska's extreme cold (Climate Zone 7) creates unique insulation requirements that justify premium spray foam pricing
2. **Code Requirements**: BEES 5-star minimum (89 points HERS scale) drives demand for high-performance insulation
3. **R-Value Requirements**: R-49 to R-60 attics, R-21 to R-30 walls - significantly higher than lower-48 states
4. **Ice Dam Problem**: Major pain point for Alaska homeowners, spray foam is proven solution
5. **Energy Loss**: Up to 40% energy loss from air leakage - spray foam's air sealing is critical value proposition
6. **Market Size**: AHFC finances ~20% of residential construction, setting quality standards
7. **Federal Funding**: $74.6M in rebates available (up to $1,600 for insulation) - major market driver
8. **Tax Credits**: 30% tax credit through 2032 improves ROI significantly
9. **Contractor Expertise**: Alaska installation requires specialized knowledge of cold-weather application
10. **Service Area**: Anchorage is hub, but Fairbanks, Wasilla, Palmer, Juneau all viable markets
