## Link Strategy Recommendations

### High-Value Links to Include
1. **AHFC BEES page** - Primary authority for Alaska code requirements
2. **DOE Building Energy Codes - Alaska** - Federal authority reference
3. **UAF Cooperative Extension Building Challenges** - Educational authority
4. **CCHRC Arctic Wall** - Research-backed alternative methods
5. **UpCodes Alaska Energy Code** - Accessible code reference

| CCHRC | "Cold Climate Housing Research Center" |
| UAF Extension | "University of Alaska Fairbanks Cooperative Extension" |

### External Links Per Section
- **Introduction**: 1-2 authority links (AHFC, DOE)
- **Climate Zones**: 2-3 links (DOE, IECC, UpCodes)
- **R-Value Requirements**: 2-3 links (AHFC manual, Insulation Institute)
- **Vapor Barriers**: 2 links (Building Science, DOE)
- **Spray Foam**: 1-2 links (CCHRC, industry source)
- **Compliance**: 2-3 links (AHFC AkWarm, compliance guides)

### Total Recommended External Links
- **Minimum**: 8-10 quality external links
- **Optimal**: 12-15 external links
- **Maximum**: 18-20 (avoid over-linking)
